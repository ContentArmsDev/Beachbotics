About Beachbotics

We are Beachbotics, a passionate team of young innovators and engineers. Our team is comprised of students who love building, programming, and competing in the exciting world of robotics. Our mission is to inspire and engage with STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) through hands-on experiences and teamwork.

FIRST LEGO League Competition

The FIRST LEGO League (FLL) is an international robotics competition that encourages children and teenagers to think like scientists and engineers. Each year, FLL releases a new challenge based on a real-world scientific topic. Teams must design, build, and program a robot using LEGO Mindstorms technology to complete tasks and score points on a themed playing field.

This year's challenge is all about renewable energy solutions, and we are excited to showcase our innovative projects and robotic solutions at the upcoming regional competition!
